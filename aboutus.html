About reUNITE
A reUNITE website is a valuable resource for pet owners who are searching for their lost companions. This website provide a central place to post information about lost pets and connect with people who may have seen them.reUNITE envisions a world where all people recognize the intrinsic value of animals and consistently make choices that demonstrate compassion and respect.
We Simplify the Search for Lost Pets
An estimated 10 million pets go missing every year, but without a central location to report them lost or found, many may never be reunited with their families.
reUNITE wants to keep pets where they belong: at home with you. We help missing pets with just one photo. It's fast, free, and easy to use.
About reUNITE Love
Love changes everything. When you share your life with a pet you know what it means: cozier naps, longer morning walks, warmer welcome homes every time you walk in the door.
reUNITE harnesses the power of love to drive us every day to make communities and pet families healthier, stronger, and closer.
